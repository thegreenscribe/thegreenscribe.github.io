Reading

Do you need to get something read, understood, and snappily summarized?  We do that! Do you need someone to read your book, story, screenplay, research summary and give you notes?  We can help! When your project evolves beyond reading and minor notes to the need for in-depth services, booking a second service includes a returning client appreciation price.

Alpha Reading

Reading your unedited rough draft, discussing plot, dialogue, tracking of metaphors, timelines, reader first impressions, style, structure, does not pay attention to grammar, spelling, punctuation, and other concerns for the beta reader, editor, and proofreader to address.

Beta Reading

Reading of your edited draft, notes on the grammar, spelling, punctuation, facts, tracking of time and tense, and suggestions implemented by an alpha reader.
